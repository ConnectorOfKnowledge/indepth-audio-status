Under the Hood

Enterprise-Grade Architecture
Built on Next.js 15 with TypeScript — the same technology stack used by Hulu, Nike, and Notion. Fully typed codebase across 48 source files with a 235-line custom design system built specifically for the In Depth Audio brand. Every component engineered for performance, accessibility, and scalability.
Cinematic User Experience
Custom scroll-driven animations throughout the site using a professional animation engine. Atmospheric visual effects, smooth transitions, and an immersive interface designed to match the premium nature of Legacy Audio products. This is not a template — every interaction was crafted from scratch.
100% Product Data Accuracy
Every product price for all 31 Legacy Audio products was individually cross-checked against legacyaudio.com and verified for accuracy. 10 specification items were flagged where Legacy's own published sources contain discrepancies — documented and ready for Legacy to resolve.
Real Product Photography
297 source images processed from Dave's photo library. The 95 best images were selected, professionally optimized (268 MB reduced to 11 MB without quality loss), and mapped across all 31 products. Every product page shows real Legacy Audio photography.
SEO & Search Engine Optimization
Structured data (JSON-LD) embedded on every page so Google can display rich product results, business info, and breadcrumb navigation directly in search. Static page generation for fast load times. Fully responsive across all devices.
E-Commerce Infrastructure
Full product catalog with advanced filtering by category, price range, and product tier. Finish selector with live pricing. Lead capture forms wired and ready. Add to Cart on every product. Shopping cart and Stripe checkout are the next phase.
Research-Driven Decisions
10+ dedicated research documents covering animation technology, CMS architecture, payment processing, competitor analysis, and UX best practices. Every major technical decision backed by research, not guesswork.
